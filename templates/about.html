Home Equity Loan Defauter App

This app mutually design for Home Equity Loan Default Detector, here you can find if the user is going to be a defaluter or not defaluter base on previous data.
This model is trained on a dataset kaggle, which our model can predict on 94% accuracy. Random Forest Classification Algorithm is used for this dataset.

How AI predicts and prevents delinquency By comparing data and trends, AI helps lenders predict customers who will become delinquent as early as 12 months in advance. This early warning enables loss mitigation techniques to save customer relationships. These can include reaching out to customers with the means to pay but who forgot to make a payment or customers who mistakenly believe theyâ€™ve set up automatic payments and have fallen behind for several cycles. This also presents opportunities to help those who may be struggling after a sudden job loss or other circumstances. AI helps to segment clients into risk stages as they tend to overlap. For instance, at origination there is the potential for delinquency in the first three to six months, so that group is closely monitored. Existing customers are monitored throughout the lifecycle, and some may become unemployed. Customers with previously good payment records may be offered relief, such as balance transfers or lower interest for finite payment plans. Using AI to determine who is 30 and 60 days behind on payments allows management to prevent them from becoming seriously delinquent at 90+ days past due and going into collections.
